Super Junior

Super Junior (슈퍼주니어) is a South Korean boy band debuted on November 6, 2005, under SM Entertainment. Having originally debuted with twelve members—and a thirteenth who followed soon after—there are nine active members currently: Leeteuk, Heechul, Yesung, Shindong, Eunhyuk, Donghae, Siwon, Ryeowook and Kyuhyun.

They were the first K-pop group I became a fan of, back in 2011—they're also the main reason I sticked to this genre of music until this day. Even if they're not currently my favorite group, they will always have a huge place in my heart.

Wanna One

Wanna One (워너원) was a South Korean boy band debuted on August 7, 2017, first under YMC Entertainment and later by Swing Entertainment. The group was formed through the second season of the reality show Produce 101, with its eleven members chosen by the korean population. The group consisted of: Yoon Jisung, Ha Sungwoon, Hwang Minhyun, Ong Seongwoo, Kim Jaehwan, Kang Daniel, Park Jihoon, Park Woojin, Bae Jinyoung, Lee Daehwi and Lai Kuanlin.

Even if they weren't the second group I became a fan of over the years, they were my favorite K-pop group from 2017 until 2019, where they disbanded after their 1-and-a-half year of promotions ended. Even if they're not together anymore, they still have a huge part of my heart, and I'm still a fan of some of the members individually.

Stray Kids

Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈) is a South Korean boy band debuted on March 25, 2018, under the label JYP Entertainment. The group was formed through a reality show in 2017 by the same name, in which 9 contestants had to prove their worth as a team to be allowed to debut as a group. With one of the members leaving in 2018, the team is currently formed by 8: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin and I.N.

Having become a fan of them relatively late, they are currently my favorite K-pop group.

Enhypen

Enhypen (엔하이픈) is a South Korean boy band debuted on November 30, 2020, under Belift Lab, a joint venture between CJ ENM and Hybe Corporation. The group was formed through a reality show in 2020 called I-Land, and is composed of 7 members: Jang Jungwon, Lee Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Park Sunghoon, Kim Sunoo and Ni-ki.

Without really planning on stanning another group anytime soon—not with my attention already divided by the previous three—they became my most recent treasure the past few months.
